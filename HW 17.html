The Problem

Those experiencing homelessness are more exposed to chronic illness, injuries, suidical ideation, and violence than others.

>2,000 People

Every night, more than 2,000 people in the greater Kansas City area sleep on the street, in their cars, or in homeless encampments.

8,471 Students

An estimated 8,471 public school students experience homelessness in recent years.

47 Years Old

The average life expectancy of a U.S. citizen is 79 years old, but for a person experiencing chronic homelessness, that number drops to just 47 years old.

We work to provide the most vulnerable population in Kansas City with access to quality holistic health care and social services.
